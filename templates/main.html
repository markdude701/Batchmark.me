Our Quick How-To

No Need to make an account. Just hit the upload button on the top right, or if you are on a mobile device, scroll all the way to the top and you'll see it. The site will continute to prompt you for a series of uploads. The first page is the Watermark upload. This is where you will upload the watermark image you want to be displayed in the other image. The second page is the photo upload page. Here, you will upload the photos you want to be watermarked.

What else?

The last page will take you to the placement page. Here you will see you uploaded file names on the top, and make a selection on where you would like you watermark to be on the image. If it tells you to try again, hit the upload button again, and try the process a little bit slower. A funky bug with HTML/PYTHON. But after the submit button is pressed on the placement page, you will be redirected to the process page, which should prompt you to download your requested image. Any questions should refer to the FAQ.
