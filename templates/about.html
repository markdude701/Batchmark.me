FAQ

Why should I used this site compared to others that do the same thing?

This site is made to be straight to the point. Watermark your photos and go on with what you were doing. No need to make an account or pay or any of that garbage.


Why was this made?

This was made for a semester long assignment for Digit 400 at Penn State Behrend, taught by Dr. Aaron Mauro. I felt this class was about the technology and actually understanding how everything all ends of web developement are made, from sever management, html, scipting in Python on the backend, and the idea of creating an application that actually has some practical use.


Is it secure?

Of course! The connection you are using, assuming you were able to connect through HTTPS, uses a 256 bit encryption when sending information to our server. The images are only saved for a short time for processing, and then disposed of.


Why does it keep telling me to try again?

I had some issues in with keeping the photo upload variables sticking with the page's function, if that makes sense. By the time your session gets to the placement page, where it needs all of the variables, I have coded it to tell you to try again, instead of giving you a nasty Error 500 on your screen, when it has problems finding a variable.
