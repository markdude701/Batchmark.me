Production

During the Spring 2017 semester, I was given the opportunity to intern at HAPPI 92.7 in Erie, assisting in projects that help betterment of the company. During this time, I have also taken the Radio Practicum class with Mr. Thomas Deau, and was given the opportunity to host my own radio show, called The Vinyl Version. It was all quite an enjoyable experience.


Also during this time, I was able to presue an interest and audio editing and recording. I was able to study the concepts of microphones, electronic waves, ProTools, Adobe Audition, drum tracking, side-chain compression, equalization, and rythem. The focus of this class was to create professionally styled, quality material. I was also able to record my radio shows through Adobe Audtion, and learn to effectively equalize audio and voice tracks.
